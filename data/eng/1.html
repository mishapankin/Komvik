Financial Audit

Financial audit of operations on conformity to legislation provisions and interest of owners are necessary to reveal the documents containing possible contempt of law. The basic principles of financial audit are legitimacy, objectivity and independence. The purpose of a financial audit is to ensure that the auditors are functioning correctly. A number of tasks are set to achieve this goal, each of which reflects some aspect of the company's activities.
In particular, the complex of works may include:
  • Control over the targeted use of funds allocated to recipients
  • Obtaining information on the implementation of projects in the process of their implementation
  • Prevention of inappropriate use of funds of received funds by recipients
